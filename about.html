The Beginning

A Journey Into the Mist

Wild Rainier began not in a boardroom, but on a windswept ridge above Paradise, where the clouds parted to reveal the sprawling emerald expanse of Washington's wilderness. That moment — the kind that steals your breath and rewires your soul — became the seed of something bigger.

Founded in 2018 by a small collective of guides, photographers, and conservationists, our mission is simple: to connect people with the raw, unfiltered beauty of the Pacific Northwest. We believe that the wild is not a place to be conquered, but a companion to be walked beside.

Today, we curate experiences that honor the land — from the moss-draped cathedrals of the Hoh Rainforest to the icy grandeur of Mount Rainier's glaciers. Every trail we share, every story we tell, is rooted in respect for the ecosystems that make Washington one of the most breathtaking places on Earth.

Milestones

Our Journey

The path that brought us here — from a single ridge to a community of thousands.

2018 THE BEGINNING

Wild Rainier is founded by a small group of guides who shared a vision: to make the wild accessible, respectful, and unforgettable.

2020 EXPANSION

We expanded beyond Rainier to include the Olympic Peninsula, North Cascades, and the Columbia River Gorge — bringing more of Washington's wild to the world.

2022 COMMUNITY

Our community surpassed 2,500 hikers. We launched our conservation initiative, partnering with local organizations to protect fragile alpine ecosystems.

2024 IMPACT

We planted over 5,000 native trees, restored 12 miles of trail, and launched our youth wilderness program — inspiring the next generation of stewards.

2026 TODAY

Wild Rainier is now a trusted name in Pacific Northwest hiking — rooted in community, guided by wonder, and always looking toward the next ridge.
