Trail Tips

Before You Go

Essential advice to make your hike safe, enjoyable, and respectful of the wild.

Check Weather

Mountain weather changes quickly. Always check the forecast and be prepared for rain, wind, or sudden temperature drops.

Pack the 10 Essentials

Navigation, headlamp, sun protection, first aid, knife, fire, shelter, extra food, extra water, and extra clothes. Don't leave without them.

Leave No Trace

Pack out everything you pack in. Stay on trail, respect wildlife, and leave the wild as beautiful as you found it.
